Annie's Favourite Movies 2024

Here are my favourite movies of the last year...

1. Dune 2

This movie in an epic dystopian sci-fi sequel with an intruiging love triangle built into the action. Even better than the first one. Five stars. Could have watched another 3 hours of it.


2. The Long Game

I can't quite remember the title but this is a heartwarming movie about te social divide between Americans and Mexicans living in Texas in the 20th century. The story follows a group of Mexican golfers who want to compete and join a White American golf club. Ostrocised by both the White American community and the Mexcian community over the river, this is a sure tear jerker.


3. Horizon

This was the start of the full western immersion Kevin Costner promised. Lacking a bit of plot direction but hopefully all will become clear in the next 4 movies he has planned for us. Visually stunning with great actors and score.
